ADMISSION DETAILS

ADMISSION PROCEDURE

  • Interested Candidates can obtain Application forms from Admission Office at IMS, Ghaziabad. Candidates can also fill the Online inquiry form by clicking on the following link:-
    http://www.imsuc.ac.in/apply-online-query.php.

  • Registration Process starts from January 2019

  • Candidate seeking admission in M.Sc. Biotechnology is required to meet the admission counselor in the institute premises.

  • Admission is confirmed on the basis of merit & interaction.

ELIGEBILITY CRITERIA

Admission to the M.Sc. Biotechnology course is open to the graduates of recognized university satisfying the following conditions:

  • The candidate should have obtained the Bachelors degree in Biotechnology/ Biochemistry/ Zoology/ Microbiology/ Botany/ Chemistry/ Agriculture.

  • The candidate should have secured at least 50% (45% for SC/ST) in aggregate at graduate level university examination.

  • Candidates appearing for B.Sc. final year examination or awaiting their results are also eligible to apply. Their candidature, however, shall be considered, subject to the clearance of the qualifying examination.

SELECTION PROCEDURE

  • The admission to the M.Sc. Biotechnology program will be done on the merit (based on the aggregate marks at graduate level) declared by the CCS University.

    MASTER OF SCIENCE (BIOTECHNOLOGY)

    Biotechnology is a broad discipline in which biological processes, organisms, cells or cellular components are exploited to develop new technologies. Now, Biotechnology is one of most expanding branch of science. It signifies the theoretical, applied and general skills along with scientific and technology disciplines. A post graduation course in biotechnology provides excellent critical thinking skills, a strong commitment to pursuing rigorous research training in subject area and collegiality.


    MASTER OF SCIENCE (BIOTECHNOLOGY)@IMS

    M.Sc. Biotechnology at IMS Ghaziabad- University Courses Campus is basically two year post graduation program which is affiliated to CCS University, Meerut with an intake of 30 seats that comprises of total four semesters.
